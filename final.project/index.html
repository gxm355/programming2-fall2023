About Kanika Malhotra

Kanika Malhotra is a very prolific artist based out of Chicago, Illinois. Since she was young, she has always had a passion for depicting her thoughts in the form of painting. Growing up in India, she admired the ornate details of Ancient Indian Art and natural illustrations, along with looking up to many distinguished European artists. Malhotra's artist style takes elements from numerous European styles of art, from Cubism to Renaissance era Impressionism, and fuses it with Indian styling to create a unique art identity.
